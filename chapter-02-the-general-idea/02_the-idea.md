# The Idea of this project

The idea is to build a domain specific blockchain!  But first of all, two questions.  What is a domain specific blockchain, and do we really need one?  There have been numerous papers which we will review in chapter 4.

The idea of "poker over the phone", whereby parties can play on insecure channels and with trustless parties, has been described in the paper "Mental Poker".

For our project to become a thing, we must also include a few other components which make it perfect for a blockchain application.  Poker is fundamentally about money.  Remove the financial incentives of poker and it gets boring really quickly.  Stablecoins have become the backbone for adoption, with USDC and USDT taking market share.
